Blood Map is a revolutionary mobile application designed to connect blood donors with individuals in urgent need of blood transfusions.

The app employs a sophisticated notification system that instantly alerts nearby users when a blood requirement is posted within their vicinity. This feature ensures that potential donors are quickly informed of urgent situations, facilitating prompt and lifesaving responses. In cases where immediate assistance is not available locally, the notification system seamlessly expands its reach, broadcasting alerts to a wider audience. This innovative approach enhances the chances of finding a suitable blood donor by leveraging a broader network of potential contributors. With its user-friendly interface and real-time notification capabilities, Blood Map is a vital tool in the effort to streamline and optimize blood donation processes, ultimately saving lives in critical situations.
